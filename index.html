GovHack is an annual open data competition. Teams work together over 46 Hours to explore, mash up, ideate and communicate concepts using open Government data.
GovHack Gold Coast will be held at Griffith University, July 29-31.

A brief intro to

GovHack

All sorts of people join GovHack, including entrepreneurs, developers, data analysts, engineers, designers, digital media creators and academics.
The best teams have a mix of skills, including those for story boarding ideas, building prototypes and creating a video pitch.
GovHack provides an opportunity for Governments and industry to support our innovative communities through sponsorship and the release of new open Government data.
